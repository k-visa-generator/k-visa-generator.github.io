## 6. FAQ ğŸ™‹â€â™€ï¸

**Q. Can I open a bank account before I get my Residence Card?**
A. Some banks might allow a restricted account (with transaction limits) using just your Passport + Certificate of Admission, but most will require the Residence Card.

**Q. What if I move to a new house?**
A. You MUST report your change of residence within **14 days** of moving. You can do this at the local community center (Dong-office) or online at HiKorea. (Failure to do so results in heavy fines!)

**Q. When can I start a part-time job?**
A. You can legally work only *after* receiving your Residence Card AND getting **'Permission for Part-time Work'** from immigration (with your professor's sign-off). (For D-2, usually allowed after 6 months, but rules vary by GPA and Korean proficiency).
